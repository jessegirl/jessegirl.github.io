Fromac was born on 1994. The union of manifacturing reality of leather machines and the great experience and competence of people who treat in this field gives life to a new "idea". The support of technical assistance allows Fromac to complete this project: a company that even if just born it has great experience in the leathergoods and upholstery production.

In the 2002 in the new site in Tricesimo (UD) Fromac make use of the leathergoods know-how together with furniture and footwear know-how and dedicates to development and personalization of machines. This is now Fromac's aim: realize special machines just dedicated for leather articles of these kind of branches.
